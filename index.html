LEGAL NOTICE
Subject: Legal Notice calling upon you to forthwith cease verbal abuse, mental harassment, self-harm threats, intimidation, alcohol consumption, alcohol-induced disturbance and related unlawful conduct.
Sir,

Under instructions and on behalf of my client Mr. Sangaraju Vikram Varma, aged about 29 years, son of yourself, residing at 20-1-416/1b, Subhash Nagar, Korlagunta, Tirupati - 517501, I hereby issue you this Legal Notice as under:

1.
That you are the father of my client and husband of Mrs. Pushpa Kumari Muddaloor, and the parties are members of the same family/household. My client states that he has always acted with concern, patience, responsibility and restraint towards you and towards the welfare of the family.
2.
That for a considerable period, you have been habitually and excessively consuming alcohol, and under its influence, you have been repeatedly subjecting my client and other family members to verbal abuse, intimidation, mental cruelty, emotional harassment and severe disturbance of domestic peace.
3.
That my client states that you have, on several occasions, refused to take proper food and have consumed alcohol instead, thereby worsening your health condition and causing repeated anxiety, fear, distress and disturbance to the family members.
4.
That my client further states that on multiple occasions you have threatened to commit suicide and/or self-harm, thereby causing fear, panic, emotional trauma and serious mental agony to my client and the family members. Such threats have created alarm and have compelled the family to respond under distress and fear for your safety.
5.
That whenever your health condition deteriorates due to alcohol consumption, the family members, out of concern and responsibility, have taken you to hospital for medical assistance. However, you have repeatedly refused to cooperate with medical treatment, insisted upon discontinuation/removal of saline and other medical care within a short span of approximately 10 to 15 minutes, disturbed hospital staff, returned home, and thereafter resumed alcohol consumption.
6.
That the aforesaid conduct has resulted in a repeated harmful cycle, endangering your own safety and health while also causing grave disturbance, fear, humiliation, emotional stress, mental harassment, insecurity and loss of peace to my client and the family.
7.
That my client and the family members have repeatedly attempted to handle the matter within the family and through medical assistance, but your continued consumption of alcohol, refusal to cooperate with treatment, abusive conduct and self-harm threats have made the situation unbearable and unsafe for the household.
8.
That my client states that your conduct has also caused serious distress to Mrs. Pushpa Kumari Muddaloor and other family members. The female members of the household, including Mrs. Pushpa Kumari Muddaloor, reserve their independent remedies under the Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act, 2005, including remedies for verbal, emotional, mental and economic abuse, as may be available in accordance with law.
9.
That your acts, if continued, may attract appropriate civil, criminal, preventive and other legal proceedings, including but not limited to action under Section 351 and Section 352 of the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, 2023, preventive proceedings under Section 126 and other applicable provisions of the Bharatiya Nagarik Suraksha Sanhita, 2023, remedies available to the female members under the Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act, 2005, and emergency medical/legal intervention under applicable provisions of the Mental Healthcare Act, 2017, including Section 94, where circumstances so require.
10.
That my client specifically states that your repeated alcohol consumption is the root cause of the recurring disturbance, abuse, hospital emergencies, self-harm threats and mental agony caused to the family. 11.

(a)
immediately stop consumption of alcohol at once and refrain from purchasing, procuring, storing or consuming alcohol in any form;
(b)
immediately stop all verbal abuse, mental cruelty, emotional harassment, intimidation and insulting behaviour towards my client, Mrs. Pushpa Kumari Muddaloor and other family members;

immediately stop making threats of suicide, self-harm, violence, intimidation or any other threat intended to create fear, panic, alarm or emotional pressure upon my client and the family members;
(d)
immediately stop causing nuisance, disturbance, harassment or breach of peace at home, in hospitals, towards attending medical staff, neighbours, relatives or any other person connected with the family;
(e)
fully cooperate with proper food intake, medical evaluation, psychiatric assessment, de-addiction treatment and all professional medical intervention as may be advised by qualified doctors, hospitals, counsellors or de-addiction specialists;
(f)
not endanger yourself, my client, Mrs. Pushpa Kumari Muddaloor, other family members, hospital staff or any third person by intoxicated conduct, refusal of treatment, self-harm threats, abusive behaviour, panic creation or repeated crisis situations;
(g)
not compel, threaten, manipulate or emotionally pressurize my client or family members through alcohol consumption, threats, abuse, refusal of treatment or repeated emergency situations; and
(h)
maintain peace, dignity, discipline, safety and lawful conduct within the household at all times.
